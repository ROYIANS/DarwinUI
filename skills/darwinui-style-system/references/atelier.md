# Atelier

## Core Tone

Night studio, premium, dramatic hierarchy, curated presentation.

## Tokens

- Dark base: `#0a0a08`, `#111110`, `#1a1a17`
- Light text: `#f0ece0`, `#e8e3d4`
- Gold accents: `#d4a84b`, `#b8892e`
- Secondary accents: `#c8d44b`, `#c84b2e`
- Rules: `rgba(240,236,224,0.08~0.15)`

## Typography

- Display: `DM Serif Display`
- Structural heading: `Syne`
- Labels/meta: `Syne Mono`
- Chinese body: `Noto Sans SC`

## Component Vocabulary

- Hero with strong intro presence
- Kicker, Badge, Pull Quote, Callout
- Feature Card, Figure, Chapter Opener
- Annotation, Footnote, Breadcrumb

## Constraints

- Keep one premium focal point per section.
- Motion can be elegant (fade/reveal), never noisy.
- Do not use many saturated colors at once.
