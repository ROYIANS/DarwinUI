# Editorial Ink

## Core Tone

Editorial, rational, restrained, readable-first.

## Tokens

- `--ink: #0f0e0c`
- `--paper: #f5f0e8`
- `--paper2: #ede8dc`
- `--accent: #c8391a`
- `--dim: #7a7468`
- `--rule: rgba(15,14,12,0.12)`

## Typography

- Body and titles: `Noto Serif SC`
- Labels and data: `Space Mono`
- Secondary sans: `Noto Sans SC`

## Component Vocabulary

- Kicker, Lead, Pull Quote
- Spec Block, Dark Card
- Stat Card, Data Bar
- Table, Timeline, TOC, Do/Don't

## Constraints

- Accent color is sparse and intentional.
- Motion should be subtle and low interference.
- Avoid flashy gradients, heavy shadows, and decorative overload.
